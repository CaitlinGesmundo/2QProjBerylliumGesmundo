Crohn's Disease vs. Ulcerative Colitis
Crohn's Disease Ulcerative Colitis
Location Anywhere Colon only
Continuous Inflammation Have healthy areas in between Does not have healthy areas in between
Which Layers Are Affected Sometimes has mouth sores Does not have mouth sores
