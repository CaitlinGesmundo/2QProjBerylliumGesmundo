Inflammatory Bowel Disease: Symptoms, Treatments, Differences Between Types

Diagnosis Process

Truthfully, how you get diagnosed varies from one individual to another. It entirely depends on the person!

It's always best to talk to a doctor. That being said:

Research in the Philippines is limited, and oftentimes specialists are not available. If you have noticed that you share some of these symptoms, talk to an expert as soon as you can in order to get treated quicker.

Usually, IBD patients get sick and hospitalized. This is when most patients get diagnosed. It can take months or even years.

It can be a long and difficult process. But life always has its ups and downs.

Throughout it all, never forget: you'll get through this :)
